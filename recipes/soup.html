Soup Recipe

Description

In this recipe we will show you how to make one of the simplest and most delicious soups out there made from one of each vegetable you have in your kitchen,of course,you can make any changes you could possibly want in terms of numbers,say for example that you love carrots then you could put two instead of one.But we advise you to follow the recipe strictly if this is your first time cooking this dish.

Ingredients

  • one piece of beef or chicken
  • one onion
  • five or six cloves of garlic
  • one carrot
  • one potato
  • one zucchini or courgette (whatever you call it)
  • two medium pieces of pumpkin if you're in the season
  • a few green peas (optional)
  • salt
  • pepper
  • coriander
  • creme fraiche

Steps

  1. cut the vegetables into small pieces
  2. put some oil in a pressure cooker then add the piece of meat when heated and let it cook for 5 minutes on low heat
  3. cut vegetables in the meantime if you haven't done it already
  4. put all the vegeatables in the pressure cooker
  5. add some salt one teaspoon of pepper and 3 teaspoons of coriander
  6. mix the lot and let it simmer from 7 to 10 minutes on low heat
  7. pour boiling water until it barely covers the vegetables
  8. Close the pressure cooker and let everything cook on medium heat untilpressure cooker start making some noise, when that happens, switch to low heat and let it cook for approximately 25 minutes
  9. when time is over,let the pressure cookker blow off all the steam then open it
  10. add some creme fraiche
  11. mix everything until smooth
  12. it it's too heavy add some more boiling water gently and slowly mix again then check the texture

And voila your veggie soup is ready to be devoured!
