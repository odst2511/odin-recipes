Chocolate Cake Recipe

Description

Welcome to our website!This time, we're going to explore the depths of sweet chocolate flavor with our amazing non-layered chocolate cake.Now remember that the goal of our website is to provide easyand quick recipes? Well,that is not going to change today!This non-layered cake topped with chocolate takes one hour at most to prepare. Nothing fancy or classy,just your simple rainy afternoon dessert!

Ingredients

    For the cake:
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1/2 a teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla
  • 3/4 a cup of oil
  • 3/4 a cup of warm milk
  • 2 tablespoons of warm liquid coffee
  • 1 and 3/4 of all-purpose flour
  • 3 tablespoons of cacao
  • 20g of baking soda
  • 3/4 a cup of whatever nuts you prefer
    • For the topping:
  • 100g of your favourite chocolate
  • 1/4 a cup of milk
  • 1 teaspoon of butter
  • 100g of commercial chocolate mousse

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180 degrees
  2. Mix the eggs,sugar,salt and vanilla together until the color turns to white
  3. Add the oil,milk and coffee then mix again for a bit maybe 3 min for people who hate imprecise instructions (I used to hate them too when i was a beginner, i know how it frustrating it can be!)
  4. Add the flour,cocoa and baking soda then mix for another 3 to 4 minutes
  5. Pour your nuts into the bowl
  6. Put it in the oven for 40 minutes
  7. Meanwhile, prepare your topping by mixing all its ingredients in a water bath on low heat
  8. Once your cake is ready, get it out and let it cool down a bit before topping it with your beautiful ganache!
